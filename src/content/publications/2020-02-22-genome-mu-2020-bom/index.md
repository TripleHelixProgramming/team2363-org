This Bill of Materials (BOM) is a partial accounting of the costs of Genome Mu, our robot for the 2020 FIRST Robotics Competition game, Infinite Recharge. While not representative of actual robot development costs (see [our organization's FY2019-2020 budget](http://team2363.org/2019/11/budget-2019-2020/)), this BOM satisfies the FRC inspection requirements, and will be presented to the team's Robot Inspectors at our official events in 2020.
