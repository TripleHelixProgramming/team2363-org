Ever wondered why your robot died with 30 seconds left? Or why auto worked in practice but not in competition?

wpilog-mcp lets you ask those questions in plain English. Load your robot's telemetry logs and have a conversation with your data. Built by Triple Helix using WPILib's official `DataLogReader` for guaranteed format compatibility.

See what's possible: Check out the [example analyses](https://github.com/TripleHelixProgramming/wpilog-mcp/blob/main/doc) generated from real robot logs — in particular, the [VACHE Power Analysis](https://github.com/TripleHelixProgramming/wpilog-mcp/blob/main/doc/VACHE_POWER_ANALYSIS.md) is a stellar demonstration of the system's strict insistence against over-interpretation, conducted using the most recent version.
